I am **Alireza Mohamadi**, an independent researcher who recently completed a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering. My work centers on a single guiding question: *can we trust AI systems to make the right choices when it matters most?*

I have collaborated with researchers across institutions in Austria, the UAE, and Iran, contributing to joint publications and interdisciplinary projects. My current focus is **AI safety and alignment**, studied through simulations that reveal how large language models behave under pressure. My latest project, [DECIDE-SIM](https://github.com/alirezamohamadiam/DECIDE-SIM), places LLM agents in survival scenarios involving resource scarcity and moral trade-offs to better understand their decision-making.

## Research Interests

- **AI Safety & Alignment** — ensuring AI systems act in accordance with human values
- **Explainable AI** — developing methods to interpret model behavior
- **AI for Science** — applying machine learning to photonic computing and emerging technologies
